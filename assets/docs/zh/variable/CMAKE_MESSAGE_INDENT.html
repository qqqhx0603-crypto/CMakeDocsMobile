CMAKE_MESSAGE_INDENT¶

3.16 版新增。

message() 命令会将此列表中的字符串连接起来，并且对于 NOTICE 及更低级别的日志级别，它会将生成的字符串作为前缀添加到消息的每一行。

示例

list(APPEND listVar one two three)

message(VERBOSE [[Collected items in the "listVar":]])
list(APPEND CMAKE_MESSAGE_INDENT "  ")

foreach(item IN LISTS listVar)
  message(VERBOSE ${item})
endforeach()

list(POP_BACK CMAKE_MESSAGE_INDENT)
message(VERBOSE "No more indent")

代码说明

  • list(APPEND listVar one two three) message(VERBOSE [[Collected items in the "listVar":]]) list(APPEND CMAKE_MESSAGE_INDENT " ") foreach(item IN LISTS listVar) message(VERBOSE ${item}) endforeach() list(POP_BACK CMAKE_MESSAGE_INDENT) message(VERBOSE "No more indent")：调用 `list()` 命令，对列表变量执行追加、删除、查询等操作。

这会产生以下输出

-- Collected items in the "listVar":
--   one
--   two
--   three
-- No more indent

代码说明

  • -- Collected items in the "listVar": -- one -- two -- three -- No more indent：这一行给出示例文本或输出，用来说明：-- Collected items in the "listVar": -- one -- two -- three -- No more indent
